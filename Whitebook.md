**Status:** Conceptual / Proposed / Reference Framework

> **Notice:** ROFS-8 is an open standard proposal for structured reasoning and decision evaluation. Unless otherwise stated, terms such as equilibrium, directed relation, weighting, and convergence are used in a **proposed, conceptual, or illustrative** sense, not as claims of completed mathematical proof or fully validated algorithmic behavior.

---

## 1. Purpose & Scope
ROFS-8 helps humans and AI agents organize reasoning explicitly, making it auditable and reproducible. It focuses on evidence assessment, contradiction handling, and decision convergence. 

**What ROFS-8 is NOT:**
- It is not a finalized mathematical equilibrium theory.
- It is not a formally proven decision calculus.
- It is not a guarantee of absolute correctness.

## 2. Core Framework
### 2.1 Eight Dimensions
`D = {Human, Machine, Material, Method, Environment, Measurement, Time, Purpose}`

### 2.2 Evolution
`5M1E + WHEN + WHY → ROFS-8`  
*(Proposed as an evolution of industrial analysis for modern distributed AI systems).*

### 2.3 dim_flow
A **proposed** semantic flow model describing how decisions, constraints, and influences propagate across dimensions (e.g., `Human → Machine → Method`).

## 3. Evidence & Contradiction
- **Evidence Handling:** Explicit separation of *Observation, Interpretation, Assumption, Inference, Conclusion*.
- **Contradiction Handling:** Treating contradiction as a structural signal (missing assumptions, context shift, dimension conflict) rather than an automatic failure.

## 4. Intended Use & Limitations
Designed for reasoning frameworks, decision-support prototypes, and AI agent workflows. It is not a substitute for professional legal, medical, financial, or safety judgment. See full limitations in the canonical repository.
